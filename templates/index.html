Goschulte

Schulte tables for reading practice

Schulte tables are a type of visual search exercise that can help to improve your reading speed, attention, and peripheral vision.

Instructions

  • When you start, you will see a grid with shuffled numbers depending on your chosen size. For example, choosing a 3x3 table will shuffle numbers from 1 to 9.
  • Your eyes should be fixed in the center of the table. For a grid with an odd size, this will be one of the numbers. For a grid with an even size, it will a be a point between the 4 innermost cells.
  • You need to find the numbers in ascending order (without skipping any numbers) in a soundless count, indicating the numbers only by sight.
