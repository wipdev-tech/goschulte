Schulte tables for reading practice

Schulte tables are a type of visual search exercise that can help to improve your reading speed, attention, and peripheral vision. They consist of a grid of numbers or letters that are arranged in random order. The goal is to find each number or letter in the grid, starting from the top left and moving down and to the right, as quickly as possible.
